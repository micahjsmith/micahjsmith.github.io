2020-06-09 Fair Fight https://fairfight.com/ Today, the media reported on 7-8 hour lines for black voters in Atlanta but no lines for white voters. We can't allow the unofficial disenfranchisement of black voters -- it should be easy for everyone to vote, period. Fair Fight was founded by Stacy Abrams to promote voting protection in Georgia and other states.

2020-06-14 The Human Utility https://www.detroitwaterproject.org As a country, we need to be doing a much better job of providing basic needs for our fellow citizens.

2020-06-16 Know Your Rights Camp https://www.knowyourrightscamp.com/ Camp co-founded by Colin Kaepernick to teach to young people their rights in encounters with law enforcement. Police take advantage of their authority and for young people, knowing their rights means that they are more likely to walk away from an encounter with the police.

2020-06-19 Charles Booker https://bookerforkentucky.com/ Candidate for senate in Kentucky against Mitch McConnell. The youngest black state lawmaker in Kentucky. He is a progressive politican fighting for systemic criminal justice reform among other causes. We need to vastly increase the number of black politicians at all levels of government to build the political power to make change.
